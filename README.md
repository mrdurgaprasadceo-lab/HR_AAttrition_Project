HR Employee Attrition Analysis 📊

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes employee data to identify the key factors driving attrition.
The goal is to uncover patterns that help organizations improve retention, reduce hiring costs, and enhance workforce satisfaction.

---

🎯 Business Objective

- Measure overall attrition rate
- Identify high-risk departments and job roles
- Analyze impact of salary, overtime, and job satisfaction
- Discover demographic trends influencing turnover

---

🗂 Dataset

IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset

The dataset includes employee demographics, job details, compensation, and satisfaction metrics.

---

⚙️ Tech Stack

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

---

🔍 Key Analysis Performed

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Attrition rate calculation
- Department and role-based attrition analysis
- Salary and overtime impact evaluation
- Demographic trend analysis

---

🧠 Key Insights

- Attrition is higher in specific departments and job roles
- Employees with lower income show higher turnover
- Overtime is strongly associated with attrition
- Younger employees tend to leave more frequently

---

💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve compensation and benefits
- Monitor overtime workload
- Strengthen employee engagement programs
- Provide career development opportunities

---

📌 Conclusion

The analysis highlights actionable drivers of employee turnover and demonstrates how data analytics can support HR decision-making.
